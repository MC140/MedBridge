Our Verification Standard

Trust, But Verify.
We Do Both.

MedBridge earns no commission from hospitals. Our only revenue is your concierge fee. That structural independence is what makes our verification real.

01

JCI Database Verification

We cross-check every hospital's accreditation status directly at jointcommissioninternational.org — not from the hospital's own website. Date, scope, and any conditional findings are documented.

02

NABH Standards Review

We verify NABH status via nabh.co — India's national hospital accreditation administered by the Quality Council of India. Top hospitals hold both JCI and NABH simultaneously.

03

International Patient Dept. Audit

Direct contact with the hospital's IPD to verify English-speaking coordinators, established international protocols, transparent pricing, and communication quality.

04

Surgeon Credential Verification

MCI registration, USMLE or PLAB qualifications for Western training, PubMed publication history, current hospital privileges, and active surgical practice — all verified independently.

05

Quarterly Re-Verification

Accreditation status is re-checked every quarter. Annual review of patient outcome data where published. Continuous monitoring via direct patient follow-up with permission.

Every patient receives the hospital's JCI accreditation number before signing anything — and is actively encouraged to verify it themselves.
